Frequently Asked Questions

Which breeds are currently available at Mbinfibieh?

We primarily stock Large White, Landrace and Top Pig breeds, known for their high growth rate and meat quality..

Are the pigs vaccinated and vet-certified?

Yes our pigs are vaccinated and vet-certified for health and safety. We carry on regullar check-ups to ensure they're disease free.

Do you deliver pigs within Bamenda town?

Yes, we arrange specialized transport for bulk buyers from Mbinfibieh to Mankon, Commercial Avenue, and beyond.

Are prices negotiable for bulk buyers?

Yes we offer competitive prices for bulk buyers. Contact us directly to discuss your needs and get the best deal.

Can I visit the farm to inspect the pigs before buying

Absolutely Visitors are welcome Monday to Saturday, 8:00AM -4:00 PM. Please use the contact form below to book a viewing session.
